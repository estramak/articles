About Me

I always had a very long list of hobbies and interests, all coming and going throughout my life. It never seemed to have a specific trend though. This dot of data and growing rambles has been created because of one constant fixation within myself I have found though: the love of the human spirit and it's expression. You’ll find art, blogs, spirituality, hope, niches, poetry, and a particular level of spiraling insanity here in my little corner. It’s ever growing, ever evolving. I am enthralled with people and the physical manifestations of their lives and emotions: I hope you’ll join me in this interest, and welcome to my collection of Musings and Media <3
